The trip started after 2nd period when we put our belongings in a safe spot and hopped on board. We then proceded into downtown Memphis where Archer Malmo is located. Inside!

A picture of their employess

Once we got off the bus, we were in the heart of downtown Memphis. There was a certain city air with tall buildings all around us! We marched in a group about a block and then entered the Cotton Muesuem where Archer Malmo is located. Once inside we took an elevator a few stories up to our destination. We were greeted by an employee who welcomed us and prepped his computer for us. He started off explaining what he did and how long he'd been there. He then showed us a project sketch he had pulled up. It was very plain, but he explained that that's how most websites started off. He continued on showing us other projects including some finished projects. Along the way he added few tips and tid-bits about his past. He also explained that they hire interns and even hire people from highschool or college based on their skill level. Though he gave some pointers, he was ill-prepared and was very boring, just sayin! Once the long presentation was over, we contiued to another floor where they had other employees. It also seemed to be a more entertaining and a relaxed area. Once done we took the elevator down to the bottom floor and presented to us their studio room. Here they explained that they did projects and photoshoots here. The studio does around 150 projects a year and is one of the largest in our area. We were done with our field trip, now it was time to go on an eAt trip at cookout!
